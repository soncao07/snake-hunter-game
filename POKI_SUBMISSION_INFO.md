# Poki Submission Information

## Game Title
**Snake Hunter**

## Short Description (150-200 characters)
Classic snake game with modern graphics, power-ups, and two game modes. Campaign mode with 5 levels or Time Attack challenge!

## Full Description (500-1000 characters)
Snake Hunter is a modern take on the classic snake game featuring beautiful graphics, smooth animations, and engaging gameplay.

**Game Modes:**
- Campaign Mode: Progress through 5 challenging levels with unique wall layouts
- Time Attack: 60-second challenge mode to achieve the highest score

**Power-ups:**
- Magnet: Attracts food towards you
- Shield: Protects from one collision
- Double Score: Doubles all points earned
- Shorten: Reduces snake length instantly

**Features:**
- Combo multiplier system for skillful play
- Beautiful particle effects and animations
- Responsive design for desktop and mobile
- Smooth controls (keyboard or swipe)
- Dynamic sound effects and background music

Perfect for quick gaming sessions or extended play. Easy to learn, challenging to master!
